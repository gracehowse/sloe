Sloe — every button & card, assessed by job

Treatments drawn from one vocabulary: filled aubergine (rarest), aubergine outline, grey outline, off-white fill, text-only, ink fill, sage (semantic), red (destructive). Principle: ration the accent like Julienne — the FAB is the one filled moment; serif headings + photography carry the rest.
