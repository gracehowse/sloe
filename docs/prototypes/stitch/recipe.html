Ingredients

Serves 4
Jumbo Oats 150g
Blueberries 100g
Maple 2 tbsp
GY
Greek Yoghurt 100g
Eggs 2 large
Walnuts 30g
Oat Milk 200ml
Cinnamon 1 tsp

Method

01

Preheat your oven to 180°C (160°C fan). Grease a medium-sized baking dish with a little butter or oil.

02

In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, oat milk, maple syrup, and Greek yoghurt until smooth. Stir in the jumbo oats, cinnamon, and half of the blueberries.

03

Pour the mixture into your prepared baking dish. Scatter the remaining blueberries and the walnuts evenly over the top.

04

Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden on top and set in the middle. Let it cool for 5 minutes before serving. Drizzle with extra maple syrup if desired.
