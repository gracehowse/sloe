Per-meal macros — slot-header glance proposals

Goal: see kcal + macros for each slot WITHOUT expanding. Today only shows kcal in the collapsed state — protein-watcher can't tune their day without tapping into every meal. Five glance treatments below, ranked from calm-text to data-vis.

G1 — Inline pip row (calmest)
A row of small coloured-dot + number pips on the meta line under the slot name. No new visual primitive, just an extra row of typography. Cheapest to scan; loses the "comparative" feel of bars.
