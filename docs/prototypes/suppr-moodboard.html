An editorial cookbook that coaches you warmly

Julienne's bones — serif display, restraint, painterly illustration, a clean white-and-black canvas — wearing Lifesum's soul: an encouraging, human voice. Chic, modern, warm. Warmth comes from splashes of colour, not a cream wash. Not cartoonish, not loud, no colour-block hero.

01

Palette

Julienne's base — whites and blacks, chic and modern — with warmth coming from splashes of colour, never a cream wash. White page, soft warm-grey cards, a terracotta accent and a muted olive secondary. Amber stays reserved for over-budget (the calorie-ring rule); colour never becomes a hero.

02

Typography

Serif display + sans body. A warm editorial serif (Fraunces) for titles, recipe names and big numerals; a clean sans (Inter) for body, data and dense tracker UI where legibility wins.

Painterly illustration — one system

A single locked style — single subject, gouache-like painterly realism, soft daylight shadow, cream ground — reused for ingredients, choice tiles, empty & loading states. Initials chip is the fallback. The art-direction lock comes before the art.

Voice — the calm warm coach

Encouraging but grounded in real numbers. Never bubbly, never cold, never shaming. One personality across the app — resolves the old "calm vs coach" split.
