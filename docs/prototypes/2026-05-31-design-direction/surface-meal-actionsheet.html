BEFORE — raw ActionSheetIOS: system-blue / system-red, white-on-grey stack, no thumbnail, no macros. AFTER — branded cream sheet: grabber, one elevation, blue commit accent, reserved red, thumbnail + kcal·P/C/F header, quiet brand mark.

ENG-799 Branded action-sheet

Replace the stock ActionSheetIOS with a custom cream sheet that speaks the Edit-entry language (.sheet + .grab, kit corner radius & spacing). Kills the single biggest stock-component tell in the app and unlocks a header — thumbnail + name + kcal · P/C/F — that the native sheet physically cannot render.

ENG-795 One soft elevation

BEFORE leans on the iOS sheet's hard system shadow + grey stacked buttons. AFTER uses the single kit --elev on the sheet and each action row, with the shared .scrim dim — one elevation language across the whole product.

ENG-796 One blue commit colour

Normal actions (Edit / Copy / Share) carry the brand --blue accent in their leading icon, not iOS system-blue #007AFF. Destructive uses the reserved --red — never the win-green, never the iOS system-red.

ENG-797 One brand mark

A single quiet SUPPR mark on the sheet corner so the most-used management surface no longer reads as a borrowed OS chrome. One mark, low contrast — present, not shouting.

ENG-798 Win-moment / motion

The sheet slides up from the row with a spring; rows respond to press (:active scale). The win-colour --win stays reserved for genuine wins elsewhere — it never appears in this management menu.
