Ingredients
Serves 4
Jumbo Oats
3¼ cups
Blueberries
7 oz
Maple
¼ cup
GY
Greek Yoghurt
to serve
Eggs
2 large
Walnuts
⅓ cup
Oat Milk
1¾ cups
Cinnamon
1½ tsp
Method
01
Preheat the oven to 180°C and lightly grease a baking dish.
02
In an oven-safe dish, combine the oats, eggs, oat milk, maple and cinnamon. Mix well to combine.
03
Fold through most of the blueberries, then scatter the rest with the walnuts over the top.
04
Bake for 25–30 minutes until golden and set. Serve warm with a spoon of Greek yoghurt.
