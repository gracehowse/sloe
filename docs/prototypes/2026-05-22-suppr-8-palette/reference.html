Calorie ring — over-budget treatments

Before: when consumed > goal, the whole ring painted Orange — same colour as the bonus segment, so "over" read as "all bonus." After: pick one of two treatments in Settings → Display & extras.

Default — Brand mode
Calm Suppr blue throughout
Whether under or over, ring stays Blue. Over signals via the centre digit ("OVER 229"), not ring colour.
Opt-in — Traffic light
Green under · Red over
MFP-grade alarm signal. Distinct from bonus orange, so no more "all-bonus" confusion.
