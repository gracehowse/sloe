What the render proves

  • The mark lives small. The bloomed berry-o reads at app-icon size and inside the wordmark without explanation.
  • Plum + oat is calm, not clinical. The jewel-dark ring on warm cream feels premium and unhurried — no neon, no shouting.
  • Permission voice in-product. "Room for dinner… No rush." is the bridge and the slow pun, felt not stated.
  • The locked ring survives. Under-budget stays Sage green; engineered tabular numerals; macros use plum/damson/clay, not the ring colours.
  • Ownable accent. Frost — the berry's bloom — is a colour no competitor in the lane holds.
