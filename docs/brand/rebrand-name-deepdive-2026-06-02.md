# Suppr Rebrand — Name Deep Dive

> Generated 2026-06-02 via a 25-agent workflow: 192 candidates across 12 themes, each
> collision-screened against the recipe/nutrition/food/health app lane + trademark records.
> This is a working document — nothing chosen. Top picks still require brand-manager
> rendering + formal legal clearance before commit (the step the original Suppr name skipped).

A herb that's also wisdom (Sage) is the platonic shape. With Sage dead, the job is to find the next name carrying a genuine double meaning — culinary/garden warmth on one face, guidance/clarity/care on the other — that is clean enough to own. This document ranks every CLEAN candidate, records every kill so we never re-litigate, and lands on one recommendation.

## 5. Recommendation

### Top pick: **Sorrel**
It is the truest living heir to Sage — a real culinary herb you cook *with* (food-joy, garden, homely), short and soft and human, and it ships a warm russet **colour** as a second meaning, which hands the brand a palette for free in a watercolour-and-cream system. It carries Sage's herb-wisdom halo without Sage's collision. It was the standing favourite and it *survives* this deeper screen. The one caveat is real but manageable: a formal clearance against **Sorrel Studio** (Health & Fitness app) and the Sorrel restaurant brands — likely fine given different classes/lane, but it must be pulled before commit. If it clears, it's the name.

### Runner-up 1: **Hearty**
If Sorrel snags on clearance, Hearty is the cleanest *brief-in-a-word*: a hearty meal (food-joy, comfort, abundance) crossed with warm-hearted (the coaching voice). No collision found, deeply on-positioning ("permission, not restriction"), instantly understood, easy to say and draw. Slightly less distinctive/ownable than a herb, and weaker on the "wisdom" axis — but unmatched on warmth.

### Runner-up 2: **Comfrey**
The dark-horse with the strongest *emotional* double meaning: a folk-healing herb that literally cradles **comfort**. Clean, grounded, two soft syllables, carries the healing/wise undertone Sage had. Riskier to spell/recognise than Sorrel and a touch obscure — but it's the most defensible "wise, kind, garden" name in the clean set, and pairs beautifully with "in your element."

**Honourable mentions for the brand-manager round:** Borage (encouragement folklore), Lore (distilled wisdom, ties the "what to eat next" north star), Mellow (golden, calm, food-ripeness), Damson (rich fruit-joy + jewel palette).

Judge: does it look like a place you'd want to eat and be coached?

Specifically resolve: Sorrel vs Sorrel Studio; Comfrey/Borage/Lore/Hearty common-law and TM exposure.
3. **Domain + handle check** in the same pass (.com, .app, @ on IG/TikTok — critical for the viral push).
4. Carry **Yarrow and Cress forward as unscreened** — run the same collision screen before they can join the shortlist.
