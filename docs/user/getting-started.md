# Getting Started with Suppr

**Audience:** End Users

## What is Suppr?

Suppr helps you import recipes from the web, see accurate nutrition breakdowns, plan your meals to hit your macro targets, and track what you eat.

## Creating Your Account

1. Open Suppr and tap **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email and create a password — or use **Apple Sign-In** (iOS)
3. Complete the onboarding quiz (takes ~2 minutes)

## Onboarding

The quiz sets up your personal nutrition targets:

1. **Goal** — lose weight, stay healthy, or build muscle
2. **Body stats** — sex, age, height, weight, goal weight
3. **Activity level** — sedentary to very active
4. **Plan pace** — gentle, steady, or aggressive
5. **Calorie budget** — calculated from your stats (TDEE)
6. **Macro strategy** — balanced, low-carb, high-protein, or Mediterranean
7. **Dietary restrictions** — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.
8. **Calorie schedule** — even daily calories or flexible
9. **Fasting** — optional intermittent fasting window

Your targets appear throughout the app — in the tracker, meal planner, and recipe views.

## Importing Your First Recipe

1. Find a recipe you like on any food blog (e.g. downshiftology.com)
2. Copy the URL
3. Open Suppr → **Import** (from the More tab)
4. The URL is auto-detected from your clipboard
5. Review the recipe, pick meal types (Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner/Snack)
6. Tap **Save to Library**

Your recipe now has per-ingredient nutrition data verified against the USDA database.

## Browsing the Discover Feed

The **Discover** tab shows community recipes with photos and macro breakdowns. Tap the bookmark icon to save any recipe to your library.

## Planning Your Week

1. Go to **Plan** tab
2. Choose how many days (1, 3, or 7)
3. Toggle which meals to include (e.g. skip Snack)
4. Tap **Generate Plan**

The planner picks recipes from your library that fit your calorie and macro targets. Each meal shows portion size if adjusted. Long-press any meal to swap it for a different recipe.

## Tracking Your Food

The **Track** tab is your food diary:

- **Day view** — Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner/Snack sections with "Add Food" per slot
- **Week view** — calorie bar chart + macro breakdown per day
- Add food via: **Quick Add** (manual entry), **Search** (USDA database), **Scan** (barcode), or **Previous** (re-log a past meal)
- Long-press any logged entry to delete it
- See your remaining calories — shows red when you're over budget

## Shopping List

After generating a meal plan, a shopping list is auto-created:
- Items grouped by category
- Check off as you shop
- Long-press to remove individual items
- Share via system share sheet (works with Apple Reminders)
