What changed

  • #1.1 TIGHTEN Wordmark weight nudged from font-weight: 600 to font-weight: 700, with -0.005em letter-spacing tightening. Brings prominence closer to Linear's brandmark in the top-bar without making the wordmark shout. What it duplicates / weakens: nothing — tightens visual hierarchy, no content change.
  • #1.2 SUBTRACT Removed the search icon from the top-bar nav. Suppr doesn't have a site-wide search yet, so the icon was a broken promise — clicking it either did nothing or opened a no-op interface. What it duplicates / weakens: duplicates a search affordance the product can't fulfil. Honest UX = don't show what you don't have.

DC preservation

No DC touched. Top-bar isn't part of any defended choice; this is pure execution polish.

Refused borrows considered

Considered swapping the "Sign in" + "Get started" pair for a single "Get started" CTA per Linear's sign-in-via-CTA pattern. Refused — Suppr's existing pair is more honest about the two paths (returning users vs. new users) and matches the DC12 calm-voice posture of "no tricks at the door".

Capture gaps still open

None for this surface. Both light and dark, both viewports captured.
